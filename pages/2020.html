🌸 2020 – Hoge Bare Began ❤️

Covid time – the world had paused, but for us, it was full-time calls, endless chats, and unstoppable laughter. We used to talk *every single day*, from the moment we woke up till we said goodnight. Slowly, our conversations shifted from English to namma Kannada — and that just made everything feel more *us*. Even though we couldn’t meet in person, nothing felt missing. Because the connection was so real. So pure. So perfect.

“Hoge”, “bare”, “aa maathu”, “aa vibe” — all those silly yet meaningful words started here, and they stuck around, becoming a language only we truly understood.

That first lockdown... it could’ve felt lonely or heavy. But with you in it, it felt peaceful. Warm. Like a little bubble of happiness we had built together.
